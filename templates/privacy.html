Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To authenticate you and display your expense records.
  • To generate summaries and reports within your account.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to deliver the app:

  • Google Fonts — to load typefaces. Google may log your IP address when fonts are requested.

We do not integrate advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media trackers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via the contact information provided on our site. We aim to respond to all enquiries within 5 business days.
